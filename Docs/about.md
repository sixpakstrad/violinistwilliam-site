## Biography Summary

William Samorey is presented as a Twin Cities violinist known for refined live music experiences shaped by atmosphere, emotional awareness, and storytelling.

Key biographical points:

- Performs for weddings, private celebrations, luxury events, and concert audiences throughout Minnesota and the Midwest.
- Blends classical orchestral training with a warm, expressive event-performance style.
- Creates music around the feeling of the room, from intimate ceremonies to energetic receptions.
- Maintains an active orchestral career across the Midwest.
- Serves as Concertmaster of both the Golden Valley Orchestra and Buffalo Community Orchestra.
- Performs classical repertoire, cinematic selections, and modern music.
- Studied violin performance with Annette-Barbara Vogel.
- Earned a degree in Violin Performance with honors from the University of Western Ontario.
- Has performed as a soloist, chamber musician, and orchestral performer throughout the United States.
- Performs on a custom violin by Jennifer Becker known as `The Falcon`.

## Instrument Loan Program

The About page also introduces the Winspiration Studio Instrument Loan Program as part of William's education and access work.

Core message:

- Young musicians need instruments that support their growth.
- The instrument fund helps students access properly set-up violins and violas when cost would otherwise be a barrier.
