## Page Purpose

The main page introduces William Samorey as a live violin performer and gives visitors a clear path into the site's most important actions: inquire about an event, explore the song library, learn about performance services, and understand the emotional character of the music.

The page is built around the idea of "Live Violin Storytelling" and presents William's work as personal, atmosphere-driven, and suited to weddings, private events, and concert-style experiences.
